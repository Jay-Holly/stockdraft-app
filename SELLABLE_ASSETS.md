# StockDraft Pittsburgh Beta — Sellable Assets

_Updated 2026-07-21_

## 1. Weekly Day Trader Prize Sponsorship — from $50/week
Fund the weekly free-to-play leaderboard prize. Sponsor's name on the winner announcement every week all season. Easiest, cheapest entry point for a local business.

## 2. In-App Banner Ads — from $150/month
Placement options:
- Dashboard banner (every logged-in user sees this on login)
- Draft Room banner (highest-attention moment in the app)
- League/Matchups page banner (seen every time someone checks their weekly score)
- My Team page banner (new — high-traffic roster/lineup screen)

## 3. Weekly Award Naming Rights — from $75/week
Seven built-in weekly awards, each sponsorable separately: Winner of the Week, Rookie of the Week, Diamond Hands, Lottery Hit, Sweep Week, Loser of the Week, Bench Curse. Sponsors pick the award that fits their brand.

## 4. League Format Sponsorship — custom pricing
"The SDFL Pittsburgh Division, presented by [Sponsor]" — now expandable across formats: SDFL (football), SDHL (hockey), SDBA (basketball), and SDPL fantasy-stock leagues. Each sport format now has its own franchise-identity map with division slots, real schedules, and a 4-round playoff bracket — a sponsor can own an entire league, a division, or the playoff bracket itself ("Presented by [Sponsor] Playoffs").

## 5. Team/Franchise Branding — new, custom pricing
Custom team logo upload and per-league color theming are now live. A sponsor could underwrite a "franchise sponsorship" — their logo or colors tied to a themed franchise slot, or co-branded merchandise/graphics tied to a specific team identity.

## 6. Radio/Podcast Integration — custom pricing
Built specifically for 93.7 The Fan and Audacy:
- On-air weekly winner announcements
- Dedicated segment reading top performers and league drama
- Custom signup keyword tied to the station (CITYLIMITS mechanic already built)
- Trackable, measurable, directly tied to Pittsburgh beta referral system

## 7. Beta Founding Sponsor Package
Bundled discount for first sponsors in during Pittsburgh beta — locked-in rate before the platform scales to more sports/formats nationally.

## 8. Data/Co-Marketing (post-beta, not for first pitch)
Aggregate behavioral engagement data once real beta numbers exist. Save for the Robinhood/Webull pitch, not local Pittsburgh businesses.
